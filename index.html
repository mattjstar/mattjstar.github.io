Greetings Friends,

Welcome back to the best weekend of your summer. You've been invited to view this website because we want you to be a part of the 6th Annual 'The Summer Party.' In order to live up to (read: wildly exceed) expectations for this extravaganza we need to know how many of you wonderful people will be joining us. As you well know, once you commit to attending 'The Summer Party' the hard part for you is over: all of your meals, drinks (prepared on Saturday night by a professional bartender no less!), entertainment, and accommodations will be taken care of.

If a weekend filled with friends, food and fun sounds exciting to you, please respond to the questionnaire linked below to let us know whether you (and any guests) will be attending, along with important things such as dietary restrictions.

The cost per person for this delightful and (likely) debaucherous all-inclusive weekend getaway at the world-famous Willowstreams Estate is $100. If you plan on attending, we'll charge you for this on Venmo. Thank you in advance, can't wait to add another one of these to the history books!

See you all soon.

What should I bring?

At the bare minimum: A bathing suit (remember the slip n' slide!), towel, and cocktail attire for Saturday night. There is plenty of space to crash (couches, floors, etc...), but we definitely encourage you to bring a sleeping bag, air mattress, bedding, and/or pillows if you have.

Just to reiterate, please do not bring any alcohol. Although we appreciate the thought it is way easier to handle everything before everyone gets here.

We encourage all talents so any musical instruments you might want to break out, fun toys if you have them, board games, etc. are also welcome!

There will potentially be a yoga class Saturday morning in the fields so bring active wear and a mat if you have it.

Transportation

By public transit: Make your way to 30th st station in Center City Philadelphia by train or bus. Depending on your time of arrival there will be a bit of carpooling back and forth. If not, take the Paoli-Thornburn Septa Regional Rail Line to either Bryn Mawr and Haverford and we'll have someone pick you up.

By car: There is plenty of space at the property to park your car.
